Membership details

The Robotics Club opens memberships to all the branches of engineering. There are two types of memberships

1. By filling the application form and payment of the entrance fee as prescribed by the chairman of the club.
2. One more way to become the member of the club is that they can submit a detailed project report on any one of the projects that they made other than what the club has officially published.

NOTE:

The detailed project report may be submitted to the technical secretary of the club. The steering body of the club shall take an appropriate decision on the membership of the students, who worked on that specific submitted project on its merit.

BENEFITS OF BECOMING A MEMBER:

Lots of hands on experience on building small working models and handling of different tools and instruments are practiced. Only the members can make use of the infrastructure provided by the club.
