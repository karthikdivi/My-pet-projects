Welcome to Robogyan

Robotics is the field that is intersection of varied engineering disciplines Mechanical engineering, Electrical engineering, Electronics & communications And Computer science. One will learn skills ranging from electronics, mechanics, controls, programming. It will be very interesting and exciting for us, the students, to experiment at this interaction on a technical, applied and design level. So by setting the club we get a chance to apply the knowledge we gain in the classroom through various lectures and other sources, in real-world conditions. So the main aim of starting the robotics club is to inculcate within ourselves the habit of Thinking of technology practically.

About Our College

JNTU Collage of engineering pulivedula has been established in the year 2006 as a constituent collage under Jawaharlal Nehru Technological university (JNTU), Hyderabad. After the trifurcation of the technical university, it came under the control of JNTU, Anantapur from the year 2008.The foundation stone was laid On 25th December 2005 in the hands of our beloved chief Minster of Andhra Pradesh, Sri (DR) Y.S.Rajashekhara Reddy garu. Since then the collage has been nourished with his Visionary guidance. The collage started its first academic year with five branches of engineering and an intake of about 300 students in the first batch. The second and third year batches also have about 300 Strength for the academic year 07-08& 08-09. The students set a record in academic and extracurricular activities.
