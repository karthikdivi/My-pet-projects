On 25 th of January 2k14 we the students of VNR VJIET belonging to different clubs came together and participated in a prestigious parade organised by the forth years with the permission granted by our college. The proceedings started with the march past followed by exhibition of various themes taken up by the respective clubs participated in the form of placards. Students showed how innovative they can be in making the content reach up to every single individual, and the importance of living was conveyed.

 we the students of IEEE chose our theme to be Education System In India.

the points which we expected the organisers to highlight  about our theme are as follows:-

 *the importance of education in our country, education is the root source for employment and empowers people to stand on their feet with due respect. in the present scenario it is like the survival of the fittest and the divine weapon which can lead the citizens forward is knowledge.

* Child labour has become one of the major reasons for letting down the literacy rate of our country. it is our responsibility to break this hindrance. a child should be sent to learn, not to earn!

* Lack of education is leading to the rise of poverty scale in our country. Illiteracy is the reason for poverty today.
