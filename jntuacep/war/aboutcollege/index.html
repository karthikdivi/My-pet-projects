JNTU PULIVENDULA STUDENTS PLACE


JNTU College of engineering pulivedula has been established in the year 2006 as a constituent college under Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad. After the trifurcation of the technical university, it came under the control of JNTU, Anantapur from the year 2008.The foundation stone was laid On 25th December 2005 in the hands of our beloved chief Minster of Andhra Pradesh, Sri (DR) Y.S.Rajashekhara Reddy garu. Since then the college has been nourished with his Visionary guidance. The college started its first academic year with five branches of engineering and an intake of about 300 students in the first batch. The second and third year batches also have about 300 Strength for the academic year 07-08& 08-09. The students set a record in academic and extracurricular activities.
